MOST POPULAR INDIAN MOVIES EVER

Not very long back ₹ 100 Crore(₹1 Billion) was a big milestone for any movie in India.This seem to have changed recently. While some sections of media claim BAAHUBALI-II as the biggest movie,others claim, DANGAL is the biggest Indian movie by revenue. They are both block-buster movies lets find-out the reason for their unprecedented success and which of the two has been more popular in India and abroad.

NATIONAL TRENDS  TIME SERIES GLOBAL TRENDS

Popularity over normalised time line

 

We measured the popularity of these two movies pan-India,and took a weighted moving average to get a smooth trendline. The trailers of both these movies were well received.Positive word of mouth after the week0(release-week) has helped both get a lot of new views and favourable media coverage.

 

Baahubali II's India Popularity

Dangal's India Popularity

 

Unlike other Indian movies their popularity is not restricted to certain regions or cities they are both pan India Block-Busters. Out of these two BAAHUBALI-II takes the lead in India,it was very well received across all major cities in India

 

Baahubali II's Global Popularity

Dangal's Global Popularity

 

These two movies have been hugely popular globally- BAAHUBALI-II was popular in regions around India and countries with big Indian diaspora,On the other hand DANGAL was more popular in all the major countries.

 

Wraping up our analysis: We now know that a positive word or mouth before and after the relase in addition to the massive media coverage made these two movies a pan India hit. BAAHUBALI-II fared better in India, where as DANGAL got slightly better response globally.
