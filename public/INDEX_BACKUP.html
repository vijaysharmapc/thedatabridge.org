Data Cycle Just like a lot of everything,data needs to pass through a few stages before it is really useful !

DATA IDENTIFICATION

Data describes a real-world situation that is important to your or your organisation. Data tells us about things, people, products, items, customers, assets, records and more. The problem is: finding out and keeping what matters to you or your application.Identifying data is an iterative process.At first, you may just know some vague, high-level details but spending time at this phase definaltely helps in the long run.

 

DATA PREPERATION

Once you know what data could help you or your organisation, we start with the data preperation process .This involves collecting, cleaning,handling inconsistent data and fianlly consolidating it.Although it looks much better than the last step ,there is still some more effort needed before you can put it to good use.This step is very important as our data strategy becomes very clear,along with the data that we have been punching along, we separate what we need from what we don't.

ANALYSIS & REPORTING

Here it is time to feel good about the work we have already done and start exploring the data and reports in order to extract meaningful insights, which can be used to better understand and improve business performance. Reporting is the process of organizing this data into informational summaries in order to monitor how different areas of a business are performing.
